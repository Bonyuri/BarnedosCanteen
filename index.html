About Our Eatery

Craving the taste of home? Barnedo's Canteen serves up delicious, home-style Filipino meals made with fresh ingredients and a whole lot of love.

Enjoy our flavorful soups, from Sinigang to Bulalo, and our classic Lutong-Ulam dishes like Adobo and Fried Tilapia. Start your day right with our satisfying Silog options, including Tapsilog.
