About Us

At Barnedo's, we believe that the best meals are the ones that remind you of home. That's why we pour our hearts into every dish, crafting authentic Filipino comfort food with fresh, locally sourced ingredients. Step into our cozy canteen and let the aroma of simmering Sinigang or rich Bulalo transport you back to cherished family gatherings.

We specialize in the soul-warming classics you crave. From the tangy delight of Sinigang to the deeply satisfying broth of Bulalo, and the comforting noodle goodness of Mami and Pares, our soups are made to soothe and nourish. Indulge in our traditional Lutong-Ulam favorites like perfectly seasoned Adobo and crispy fried Tilapia, or kickstart your day with our hearty Silog variations, including the ever-popular Tapsilog.

Our History

Founded in 2010, Barnedo's Canteen started as a small eatery serving home-cooked meals. Over the years, we have grown into a beloved local favorite, known for our authentic flavors and warm hospitality. It was recently expanded to add a tapsihan and bulalo branch then it was merge due to building concerns of the tapsihan branch. It has survived both pandemics and natural disasters such as fires but we will always keep on going!
